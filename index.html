About Us

The Sibley Fire Department proudly serves the City of Sibley and the surrounding rural areas. Our dedicated team of volunteer firefighters responds to fire calls, motor vehicle accidents, hazardous material incidents, and other emergencies 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We are committed to excellence in emergency response and community service.
