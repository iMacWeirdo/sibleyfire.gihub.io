Membership Programs

Volunteer Firefighter Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years of age
  • Valid driver's license
  • Pass a background check
  • Able to pass a physical examination
  • Live within reasonable response distance
  • Commitment to attend training sessions
  • No experience necessary - we train!

Cadet Program

Our Cadet Program offers high school students the opportunity to start their firefighting journey early! Get hands-on experience, training, and mentorship while still in school.

Cadet Requirements:

  • Must be a Junior or Senior in high school
  • Passing all academic classes (not on the failing list)
  • Valid driver's license
  • Able to pass a physical examination
  • Commitment to attend training sessions
  • Parental consent required

The Cadet Program is a great way to build skills, serve your community, and prepare for a future in emergency services!

Specific requirements may vary. Contact us to learn more about either program!
